## üí° Why This Project?

Menu overload is real ‚Äî and decision fatigue hits hard when you're hungry. Food Seer eliminates the guesswork by guiding users toward a meal that fits their lifestyle and cravings without the hassle of scrolling through endless options.

We built Food Seer to:

- ‚úÖ Reduce menu fatigue and decision stress  
- ü§ñ Provide personalized suggestions via **AI chat**  
- ‚ùì Offer a quiz-based food recommendation system  
- ‚≠ê Improve recommendations over time using user preferences  
- üõí Streamline ordering into a simple ‚Äúchoose and order‚Äù flow  
- üë®‚Äçüç≥ Give at-home meal ideas if users pass on suggestions  
- üßæ Empower staff with inventory, user, and order management tools  

Food Seer bridges **AI-driven personalization** with **restaurant efficiency** ‚Äî improving both customer experience and backend operations.

## üìà Use Case

**Scenario:** Sarah is tired after work and doesn't want to decide what to eat.

### How Food Seer Helps

1. Sarah opens Food Seer
2. She chooses between:
   - a **quick interactive quiz**, or  
   - chatting with our **Ollama-powered chatbot**
3. She instantly receives a personalized meal recommendation
4. With one click, she can place an order ‚Äî no browsing required

Food Seer transforms meal choices from a chore into a simple, enjoyable interaction.
